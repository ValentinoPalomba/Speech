**Description**:
```
Speech – Talk Timer is the discreet way to stay on time, right on your wrist.

Set your talk length with the Digital Crown, add “stops” along the way, and let a gentle tap on your wrist tell you when each checkpoint — and the end — arrive. No need to glance at a clock or break eye contact with your audience.

Built for speakers, presenters, teachers and anyone who values their time on stage.

• Set any duration up to 2 hours with the Digital Crown
• Add milestone “stops” and feel a haptic tap as you pass each one
• A clear countdown on a spotlight dial, designed for a quick glance
• Haptic + notification when time is up — even with your wrist down
• Save your talks as colourful presets and reuse them in a tap
• Works entirely on Apple Watch — no iPhone required
• Private by design: nothing ever leaves your watch

Take the stage. We’ll keep the time.
```
